<h1 class="firstHeading mw-first-heading" id="firstHeading">_glGetTexGendv</h1>
<div class="vector-body" id="bodyContent">
<div class="mw-body-content mw-content-ltr" dir="ltr" id="mw-content-text" lang="en"><div class="mw-parser-output"><p>The <b>_glGetTexGendv</b>, <b>_glGetTexGenfv</b>, and <b>_glGetTexGeniv</b> statements return texture coordinate generation parameters.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Syntax">Syntax</span></h2>
<dl><dd><a class="mw-selflink selflink">_glGetTexGendv</a> GLenum <i>coord</i>, GLenum <i>pname</i>, GLdouble <i>*params</i></dd></dl>
<p>
</p>
